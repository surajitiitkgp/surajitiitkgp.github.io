About Me

Hello! I am a PhD Candidate in Medical Image Processing at IIT Kharagpur, specializing in AI-powered medical imaging solutions. My research focuses on disease classification and developing UNet/Transformer-based segmentation models for radiotherapy planning and clinical decision-making. Under Prof. Jayanta Mukhopadhyay at VIP Lab, I've pioneered two major projects: (1) CHAVI - a comprehensive medical image databank advancing radiomics research, and (2) iMediXcare - an open-source telemedicine and digital healthcare platform, from which we adapted and deployed Matri Seva Telemedicine to improve rural healthcare access at RKM, Varanasi.
